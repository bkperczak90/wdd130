Alabama

Alabama is located in the southeastern region of the United States. The state features beautiful scenery from the Appalachian Mountains in the north to the Gulf Coast in the South. Its nickname, The Heart of Dixie, reflects its central role in Southern culture. It played a pivotal role in the civil rights movement, with landmarks like Selma, Montgomery and Birmingham holding historical importance.
